How to Bake Cookies

If you follow these instructions, you should be able to bake about 24 delicious cookies.

Step 1 - Preheat the oven to 350 F˚

Step 2 - Prepare the pan

Step 3 - Peel cookie dough and place rounds approximately 2 inches apart from each other.

Step 4 - Bake for 15 to 19 minutes

Step 5 - Let cool for 3 minutes

Step 6 - Remove the cookies from the cookie sheet or pan

Step 7 - Enjoy your cookies!
